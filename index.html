How to Play

The Chain

1Given a starting prefix
2Type a word beginning with it
3Last letters become new prefix
4Harder rounds use more letters

Scoring

Longer words = more points. Rare letters (Q,Z,X,J) give bonus.

3 lives, 5 tries per round.

Bot Strategies

Bots use suffix traps (-ness, -ly, -tion), letter traps (ending on X,Z,Q), and the hardest ones minimize your available options.
